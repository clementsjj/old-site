Are apps dead?


Did you know that Salio publicized that it cost 1/10th the cost to get someone to use their web app as opposed to getting a user to use their mobile app. This means that getting 10,000 people to try an app would cost about $40,000 in marketing costs.

Google proposes as solution with what they call a Progressive Web App built on a new mobile dev framework called Firebase. You get an almost identical app like experience on your mobile.

Google claims that of users on mobile: 13% are using mobile web pages, while 87% are using mobile apps. However, of those 87%, 80% of the users time is spent in 3 apps. Thus, developers usually spend more on distribution than they will ever see in return.

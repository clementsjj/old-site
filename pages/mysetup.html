Games

31 October 2017

This page will reference games of drinking nature or otherwise.

  • "I assume" Game

    • I learned this game in a hostel in Krakow, Poland. It was a great icebreaker, and a good opportunity to drink. The rules are simple.

    Sit in a circle (with drinks). One person starts by calling out another in the circle, and saying that they assume they did something. For example:"I assume you have had a threesome." All others decide if they agree or disagree. On the count of three, those that agree give a thumbs up, those that don't agree give a thumbs down. The person in question then provides the truth. Those who were incorrect with their assumption then proceed to drink. Everyone laughs and learns a little something new.
